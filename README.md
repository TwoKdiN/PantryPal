# PantryPal: Your Kitchen's Best Friend 🥘📋

## Project Overview
**PantryPal** is an integrated warehouse and financial management system specifically designed for the food service industry (restaurants, cafes, etc.). The platform addresses the critical challenge of supply chain mismanagement by automating inventory tracking, recipe calculation, and order processing.

The system ensures that business owners never run out of raw materials by linking real-time sales (waiter orders) directly to warehouse stock levels.

## Core Features

### 📦 Smart Inventory Management
- **Real-time Tracking:** Automatic deduction of raw materials from the warehouse as orders are placed.
- **Low Stock Alerts:** Notifications for both specific ingredients and total dish availability when they fall below a predefined threshold.
- **Supplier Management:** Streamlined tracking of incoming raw materials and stock replenishment.

### 📜 Recipe & Menu Engineering
- **Dynamic Recipes:** Define dishes by their specific ingredients and quantities.
- **Cost Analysis:** Tools to manage the financial aspects of ingredients and menu pricing.

### 🍽️ Service Optimization
- **Waiter Interface:** A dedicated digital environment for taking orders at tables.
- **Kitchen-to-Waiter Sync:** Real-time updates between the kitchen staff and the service team to ensure order accuracy.

### 💰 Financial Reporting
- **Data History:** Comprehensive logs of sales, expenditures, and inventory movements.
- **Performance Insights:** Overview of business finances to aid in strategic decision-making.

## System Workflow
1. **Warehouse Setup:** Admin inputs raw materials and sets "minimum quantity" alerts.
2. **Recipe Creation:** Dishes are linked to specific warehouse items.
3. **Operational Cycle:** - Waiter sends an order via the app.
   - The kitchen receives the order.
   - The system automatically subtracts the required ingredients from the warehouse.
4. **Alerting:** If an ingredient hits the threshold, the admin is notified to restock.
